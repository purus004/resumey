About Floriculture

Floriculture is the branch of horticulture that focuses on the cultivation of flowering and ornamental plants. These plants are grown for gardens, floral arrangements, and various aesthetic purposes.

Benefits of Floriculture

  • Enhances mental well-being by reducing stress and promoting relaxation.
  • Improves air quality by absorbing pollutants and releasing oxygen.
  • Adds aesthetic value to homes, offices, and public spaces.
  • Provides opportunities for economic growth through flower farming and floral businesses.

Tips for Beginners

  1. Start with easy-to-grow flowers like marigolds, sunflowers, and petunias.
  2. Ensure your plants get adequate sunlight and water.
  3. Choose the right soil and add compost for better growth.
  4. Regularly prune and deadhead your plants to encourage blooming.
